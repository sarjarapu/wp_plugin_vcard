About Us

Acme Dental Care blends compassionate care with the latest clinical technology to deliver preventative, restorative, and cosmetic treatments tailored to each patient.


Founded in 1998, our practice has grown through word-of-mouth and results you can see. From routine cleanings and whitening to implants and emergency care, we make dentistry approachable and affordable for families across Austin.


Our clinic features same-day crowns, digital X-rays, and sedation options for anxious patients. Flexible scheduling and transparent pricing included.

We believe every smile tells a story. Our team blends compassionate care with clinical precision to deliver treatments tailored to you.


Emergency care, cosmetic dentistry, preventative plans—crafted to fit your budget and schedule.

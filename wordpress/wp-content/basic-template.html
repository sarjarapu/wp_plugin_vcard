About Us

At Acme Dental Care, we believe every smile tells a story. Our team blends compassionate care with the latest clinical technology to deliver preventative, restorative, and cosmetic treatments tailored to each patient.


Founded in 1998, our practice has grown through word-of-mouth and results you can see. From routine cleanings and whitening to implants and emergency care, we make dentistry approachable and affordable for families across Austin.


Our clinic features same-day crowns, digital X-rays, and sedation options for anxious patients. We also offer flexible scheduling and transparent pricing because healthcare should be simple.

# Dental Practice Website Strategy - Cedar Park, TX

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive strategy for building a high-performing website for a dental practice in Cedar Park, Texas. The strategy focuses on local SEO, user experience, content marketing, and conversion optimization to drive patient acquisition and establish the practice as a trusted local healthcare provider.

---

## 1. Website Structure & Page Hierarchy

### 1.1 Core Pages (Priority: High)

#### **Home Page** (`/`)
**Purpose:** First impression, establish trust, capture attention
**Key Elements:**
- Hero section with clear value proposition
- "New Patient Special" or introductory offer
- Quick contact CTA (phone, online booking)
- Trust signals (years in practice, patient testimonials, certifications)
- Services overview with links to detailed pages
- Location and hours prominently displayed
- Emergency contact information

#### **About Us** (`/about/`)
**Purpose:** Build trust and personal connection
**Key Elements:**
- Dentist bio(s) with photos and credentials
- Practice history and mission statement
- Team member profiles
- Office philosophy and approach to patient care
- Awards, certifications, continuing education
- Office tour (photos/video)

#### **Services** (`/services/`)
**Purpose:** Inform patients about treatments, drive conversions
**Key Elements:**
- Service categories with clear descriptions
- Individual service pages (see detailed list below)
- Before/after photos (with patient consent)
- Technology and equipment used
- Insurance and payment information
- FAQ section for each service

#### **Contact** (`/contact/`)
**Purpose:** Make it easy to reach the practice
**Key Elements:**
- Contact form (multiple CTAs)
- Office address with embedded Google Map
- Phone numbers (main, emergency)
- Office hours (including emergency availability)
- Directions from major landmarks
- Parking information
- Online appointment booking widget

### 1.2 Service-Specific Pages (Priority: High)

Create dedicated pages for each major service to target specific search queries:

1. **General Dentistry** (`/services/general-dentistry/`)
   - Cleanings, exams, X-rays
   - Preventive care
   - Oral health education

2. **Cosmetic Dentistry** (`/services/cosmetic-dentistry/`)
   - Teeth whitening
   - Veneers
   - Smile makeovers
   - Before/after gallery

3. **Restorative Dentistry** (`/services/restorative-dentistry/`)
   - Fillings
   - Crowns and bridges
   - Dentures
   - Root canals

4. **Dental Implants** (`/services/dental-implants/`)
   - Single and multiple implants
   - Implant-supported dentures
   - Process and timeline
   - Cost and financing

5. **Orthodontics** (`/services/orthodontics/`)
   - Traditional braces
   - Invisalign/clear aligners
   - Retainers
   - Treatment for adults and children

6. **Emergency Dentistry** (`/services/emergency-dentistry/`)
   - 24/7 availability (if applicable)
   - Common emergencies
   - What to do before arriving
   - Pain management

7. **Pediatric Dentistry** (`/services/pediatric-dentistry/`)
   - Child-friendly approach
   - First visit information
   - Preventive care for kids
   - Parent resources

8. **Periodontal Treatment** (`/services/periodontal-treatment/`)
   - Gum disease treatment
   - Scaling and root planing
   - Maintenance care

### 1.3 Supporting Pages (Priority: Medium)

9. **New Patients** (`/new-patients/`)
   - What to expect
   - Patient forms (downloadable)
   - Insurance information
   - Payment options
   - Office policies

10. **Patient Resources** (`/resources/`)
    - Oral health tips
    - Post-treatment care instructions
    - Educational videos/articles
    - Links to trusted resources

11. **Testimonials** (`/testimonials/`)
    - Patient reviews and stories
    - Video testimonials
    - Google Reviews integration
    - Before/after patient stories

12. **Blog** (`/blog/`)
    - Regular content updates (SEO + engagement)
    - Topics: oral health tips, procedure explanations, local community news

13. **FAQ** (`/faq/`)
    - Common questions organized by category
    - Voice search optimization
    - Schema markup for rich snippets

14. **Insurance & Financing** (`/insurance-financing/`)
    - Accepted insurance plans
    - Payment plans
    - CareCredit information
    - Financial policies

15. **Privacy Policy** (`/privacy-policy/`)
    - HIPAA compliance
    - Data protection
    - Required for legal compliance

## 2. Content Strategy

### 2.1 Content Pillars

1. **Educational Content**
   - How-to guides (e.g., "How to Floss Properly")
   - Procedure explanations
   - Oral health tips
   - Myth busting

2. **Local Content**
   - Cedar Park community involvement
   - Local events participation
   - Neighborhood-specific information
   - "Dentist near me" optimization

3. **Trust-Building Content**
   - Patient success stories
   - Dentist expertise and credentials
   - Technology and innovation
   - Safety and hygiene protocols

4. **Problem-Solution Content**
   - "Do I need a root canal?"
   - "How to fix a chipped tooth"
   - "What to do in a dental emergency"
   - Treatment options for common issues

### 2.2 Blog Content Calendar (Monthly)

**Week 1:** Educational/How-to article
- Example: "5 Signs You Need a Dental Checkup"
- Target: General dental health awareness

**Week 2:** Service-specific deep dive
- Example: "Everything You Need to Know About Invisalign in Cedar Park"
- Target: Specific service keywords

**Week 3:** Local/Community content
- Example: "Cedar Park Dental Health: Common Issues in Our Community"
- Target: Local SEO

**Week 4:** FAQ/Problem-solving content
- Example: "Why Do My Gums Bleed When I Brush?"
- Target: Voice search, long-tail keywords

### 2.3 Content Optimization Checklist

For each page:
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2/H3
- [ ] Local keywords (Cedar Park, TX) naturally integrated
- [ ] Internal links to related pages
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Meta title (50-60 characters)
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 characters)
- [ ] Alt text for all images
- [ ] Schema markup (LocalBusiness, MedicalBusiness, etc.)
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Fast loading speed
- [ ] Clear CTAs

## 4. Technical SEO

### 4.1 On-Page SEO

- **Title Tags:** Include primary keyword + location + practice name
  - Example: "Best Family Dentist in Cedar Park, TX | [Practice Name]"
- **Meta Descriptions:** Compelling copy with CTA + location
- **Header Structure:** Logical H1-H6 hierarchy
- **URL Structure:** Clean, descriptive URLs
  - Example: `/services/dental-implants-cedar-park/`
- **Internal Linking:** Strategic links between related pages
- **Image Optimization:** Descriptive filenames and alt text
- **Schema Markup:**
  - LocalBusiness schema
  - MedicalBusiness schema
  - Review schema
  - FAQ schema
  - Breadcrumb schema

### 4.2 Site Performance

- **Page Speed:** Target < 3 seconds load time
- **Mobile Optimization:** Mobile-first design
- **Core Web Vitals:** Optimize LCP, FID, CLS
- **SSL Certificate:** HTTPS required
- **XML Sitemap:** Submit to Google Search Console
- **Robots.txt:** Properly configured

### 4.3 Content Freshness

- Regular blog updates (at least 2x/month)
- Update service pages quarterly
- Refresh testimonials monthly
- Update office hours/contact info as needed
- Seasonal content (back-to-school dental tips, etc.)

Hi, I'm Dr. Stimmler! DDS

While I was in dental school, I was involved in outreach organizations providing free dental treatment for underprivileged youth – both in my local community, as well as throughout California’s Central Valley and in other countries such as Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and Mexico. I have also been a part of a group of students who started a club to discuss professionalism and ethics in dentistry – a club that eventually found its way into dental schools nationwide.

During my residency at Montefiore, I was able to further refine my knowledge of cosmetic dentistry, full mouth rehabilitation, and implant dentistry. I’ve also retained a desire for continuing education, always working to ensure that my practice is on the cutting edge of modern and cosmetic dentistry.

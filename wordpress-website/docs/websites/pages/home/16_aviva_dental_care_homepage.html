The dental services we offer

At Aviva Dental Care, Dr. Arjarapu provides - general, family, cosmetic, restorative, and implant dentistry for patients of all ages using the most current and conservative treatment methods available. Whether you are need Philips Zoom ® teeth whitening, or ClearCorrect ® braces, you will be treated with the highest quality dentistry available. Our goal is to assist each patient in achieving and maintaining long term dental health and a beautiful smile.

General Dentistry

Despite brushing and flossing daily at home, unfortunately, patients sometimes develop cavities or gum disease. Seeking timely treatment for such issues is crucial, before the condition worsens, requiring intensive treatment. A semi-annual visit to our dentist enables early detection of various oral complications and provide treatment early in the disease process.

Dentures & Crowns

If you have a severely decayed tooth, broken tooth or missing tooth then ask our dentist at Aviva Dental Care - Cedar Park office about the advantages of a dental crown. The dental crowns are durable and natural-looking tooth-shaped caps fitted over any remaining healthy tooth structure or a dental implant to restore the tooth's health.
Dentures & Crowns Service

Invisalign

Invisalign clear aligner system uses a series of clear plastic, removable dental trays to correct misalignments. Show off your smile as people won't even notice you're wearing transparent Invisalign aligners when compared to the traditional metal braces options. It's easy to remove aligners and eat whatever you like. There are no wires to trap food or get in the way when you floss.
Invisalign Service

Cosmetic Dentistry

If the appearance of your smile is affecting your social life, self-esteem, and confidence then we encourage you to ask your friendly neighborhood Cedar Park dentist about cosmetic dentistry. We can straighten, whiten your teeth, and can sculpt perfect smile with our modern cosmetic treatment options that will improve your current look and help you live a healthy lifestyle.

Teeth Whitening

If you have discolored teeth from smoking or consumption certain foods or beverages leaving stains on your teeth then ask Dr. Arjarapu about in-office teeth whitening treatment option. This painless procedure brightens your teeth by several within an hour. The teeth whitening is effective on natural tooth enamel but not on restorations.

Emergency Dentistry

If you or a loved one experienced a traumatic dental injury or is suffering from a severe toothache/swelling then you need to contact the dentist at Aviva Dental Care - Cedar Park office ASAP. Urgent dental emergencies can be quite traumatic, painful and need to be dealt with immediately and our team is ready and equipped to provide emergency dentistry on the very same day.
Emergency Dentistry Service

Root Canal

Root canal treatment is necessary when the pulp (soft tissue inside your teeth containing blood vessels, nerves, and connective tissue) becomes inflamed or diseased and can be extremely painful. If your dentist determines that a root canal is the best solution for you, the procedure can typically be done with minimal discomfort and often keeps you from losing the tooth.
Root Canal Service

Dental Implants

A dental implant is a metal post that functions as a replacement tooth root. Once your dentist places the implant in the bone of the jaw, new bone forms around the implant to firmly hold it in place. Implants can also support dental restorations like crowns and bridges, just like regular teeth. They can even support full or partial dentures and give them more stability in the mouth.
Dental Implants Service

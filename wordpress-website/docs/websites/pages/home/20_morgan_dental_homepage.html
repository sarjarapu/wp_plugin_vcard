Exceptional Family Dentistry With Your Comfort In Mind

Andrew A Morgan, DDS has been providing exceptional dental care for patients of all ages in Cedar Park, Leander, and Round Rock since 2001. Dr. Morgan and his staff of professionals recognize that developing a relationship built on trust, comfort, and impeccable quality will help you achieve a healthy smile that is a reflection of you.

Whatever the reason for your visit to Morgan Dental, from teeth cleaning to emergency to a cosmetic improvement of your smile, our job is to make you feel better. We aim to complete your treatments as efficiently as possible utilizing the latest technology and state-of-the-art equipment. Our many amenities include: movies and iPod entertainment, laughing gas, conscious sedation, digital x-rays, warm neck pillows and blankets, hot towels, Wi-Fi, and a refreshment bar.

Comfort also includes a no-pressure explanation of your treatment options and a knowledgeable staff to answer your questions so you may make an informed choice about your smile. We are here to help and make you feel at ease not only during treatment but throughout your entire experience at Morgan Dental.

Why are we able deliver all of the above? Because we know our staff is well trained to provide professional excellence and we genuinely CARE. This allows us to provide quality dentistry while making excellent dental health a reality for all of our patients. Come see what comfortable, gentle dental care can do for you!

Whitening

Want whiter, brighter teeth?  Tooth whitening enhances the brightness of natural teeth.  Morgan Dental in Cedar Park offers 5 types of teeth whitening including Zoom In-Office Whitening.

Crowns

Morgan Dental offers dental crowns (caps) that preserve your teeth after removal of cavities, cracks, and other dental conditions.  Crowns build strength back into your smile and are color matched to blend in perfectly with surrounding teeth.

Veneers

Looking for a Hollywood smile?  Morgan Dental offers dental veneers which can hide imperfections under thin sheets of porcelain that are permanently bonded to the front of teeth in the smile line.

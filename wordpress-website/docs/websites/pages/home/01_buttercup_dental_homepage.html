Family Owned and Operated Since 1987

Buttercup Dental - Cedar Park Texas Dentists

Over the last decade, many dental offices have changed to a corporate model that emphasizes efficiency, productivity and profit.  Rather than follow the herd, Buttercup Dental has remained true to our core values for over 38 years.  Healthy, lasting relationships are built on honesty, compassion, a friendly staff and ethical business practices.  We will continue to place the individual needs of our patients above all else, because some things should never change.

  • Most emergencies seen same day.
  • We offer a full range of sedation techniques.
  • Children are some of our favorite patients.
  • Interest-free financing is available.
  • We invite you to come see the difference.

Feel free to call or email if you have any questions. We look forward to meeting you and your family.

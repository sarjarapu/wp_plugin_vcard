Your Orthodontic Experts in Cedar Park and the Austin Area

Our patients can choose from a wide range of orthodontic treatment methods, including Invisalign, lingual braces, and clear braces (sometimes referred to as Invisible Braces) for those who are more concerned about their cosmetic appearance during treatment. We even offer accelerated orthodontic treatment plans to help you achieve a straighter smile faster plus third-party financing options to make it easier to cover the costs of your smile transformation!

Featured Services

Orthodontics

We specialize in quality orthodontics for all ages, including Invisalign clear aligners, ceramic braces, and even accelerated orthodontic treatment plans for faster results!

Dental Implants

Healthy, long-lasting dental implants allow our patients to replace lost, missing, decayed, or damaged teeth with permanent replacement teeth that look, feel, and function just like the real thing!

Periodontics

Our tailored periodontal treatments are designed to help patients at every stage of gum disease. With gentle deep cleanings, laser treatment, targeted therapies, and more, we'll help bring your gum health (and total body health) back on track!

Replace and Restore Lost Teeth

Losing teeth is a natural part of life, and its something we all experience but missing teeth can leave you unhappy and self-conscious about your smile. With dental implants from Cedar Parks periodontics clinic, you can replace missing or decayed teeth with permanent prosthetics that look and function just like the real thing.

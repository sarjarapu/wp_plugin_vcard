What services does my smile need?

Our treatment plans are always custom-made based on the unique patient sitting in the dental chair. Our office provides a variety of general services, like cleanings, dental fillings, and teeth whitening, as well as more specialty services, like veneers, dental extractions, and Invisalign® clear aligner therapy. We also offer a myriad of restorative treatment options, like root canal therapy, dental implants, and dentures, to help our patients repair their oral health and restore their healthy, beautiful smiles.

¿Qué tratamientos dentales necesita mi sonrisa?

Nuestros tratamientos son personalizados, dependiendo de las necesidades de cada paciente. Ofrecemos tratamientos generales como limpiezas, blanqueamientos dentales y resinas, así como tratamientos especializados como carillas, extracciones y tratamientos de ortodoncia como Invisalign®. También ofrecemos implantes dentales, dentaduras y endodoncias para ayudar a nuestros pacientes a mantener una salud oral óptima.

Comprehensive Dentistry

Our team practices conservative dentistry, meaning we never recommend extensive, unnecessary treatments to our patients. When you book an appointment with our Cedar Park dentist, you can expect honesty and transparency, as we always like to explain why we recommend certain services to keep our patients as informed as possible. Our ultimate goal is to provide high-quality preventive, restorative, and cosmetic services that can meet all of the needs of your entire family.

Odontología Integral

Nuestro equipo practica odontología conservadora, significando que nunca recomendamos tratamientos extensos o innecesarios a nuestros pacientes. Cuando reserva una cita con nosotros, puedes esperar honestidad y transparencia, siempre explicamos por qué recomendamos ciertos servicios para mantener a nuestros pacientes lo más informados posible. Nuestro objetivo final es proporcionar servicios preventivos, restauradores y cosméticos de alta calidad que puedan satisfacer todas las necesidades de toda su familia.

Areas Served

Dental Salon is located just north of Brushy Creek and Avery Ranch, near the intersection of Ronald Reagan Boulevard and 1431.

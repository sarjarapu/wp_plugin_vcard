New Patients

Mission Statement

To succeed by improving the health of our community and treating others with dignity and respect.

Like many offices today, Buttercup Dental has embraced a modern approach to patient care and utilizes the latest technologies available. These advances are a wonderful improvement in the way dentistry is performed, but they will never substitute for the gentle touch of a compassionate staff. That is where we truly shine. We have a large practice but still manage to accommodate most visits within one week. If you are hurting, we do everything possible to help you right away.

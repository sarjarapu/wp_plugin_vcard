Why Choose Smile in Style?

Smile in Style About Us

To our award-winning specialist, you matter, your smile matters, and who you select to design your improved smile matters. At Smile in Style, we take a personalized approach to dentistry, with an emphasis on attention to detail, unique and conservative treatment methods, and world-class patient care. Dr. Azi and her team are committed to crafting extraordinary smiles and an unforgettable experience at affordable costs. When you visit our Cedar Park, TX dental office in the Austin area, you'll soon find out how our team is raising the bar in orthodontics, periodontics, and implant dentistry.

Wondering why you should visit us at Smile in Style for your dental care in Cedar Park? Here are just a few reasons why our patients are happy they chose us:

One-stop shop for dental treatment. Enjoy the convenience of being treated by a dual-specialist dentist, who can provide specialized care in orthodontics, periodontics, and implant dentistry, in addition to a long list of additional treatments and preventive care needs.

Cutting-edge technology. Smile in Style is proud to be equipped with all the latest cutting-edge technology, including 3D digital x-rays, intraoral cameras, and 3D modeling machines.

Flexible payment methods and affordable financing. Smile in Style offers many flexible and affordable payment plans to choose from, including no-interest, in-office financing! We also have one of the lowest down payments in the area for orthodontic treatment. The financing won't affect your credit score. Our practice is in-network with most major insurance plans, too.

Designated private treatment rooms. At Smile in Style, patients are treated in closed treatment rooms, which are safer, more discreet, and more hygienic.

Extended office hours. To make it easier to include dentistry in your busy schedule, our Cedar Park and the Austin area dental office is open for appointments on Saturdays, in addition to appointments during traditional business hours.

Consistent Care by Dr. Azi. Unlike many other offices with multiple doctor rotations or leaving during treatment, Dr. Azi will personally oversee your care from start to finish, ensuring continuity and quality.

Our welcoming team and multilingual staff. Our patients are treated with warmth, dignity, and respect. Customized treatment plans mean every patient is treated on a highly individualized level. Throughout the entire treatment process, you'll notice our team is kind, welcoming, and highly skilled. We also are proud to have on-site multilingual staff members to ensure all communications are seamless and accurate. Languages spoken at Smile in Style include English, Spanish, German, and Farsi!

Your comprehensive wellness community. When you visit us at Smile in Style, you're more than another patient at the dentist's office. You become a member of a Cedar Park wellness community a private practice working with caring partners who are each focused on creating healthy smiles and positive experiences.

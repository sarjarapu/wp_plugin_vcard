Hi, I'm Dr. Stimmler! DDS

While I was in dental school, I was involved in outreach organizations providing free dental treatment for underprivileged youth – both in my local community, as well as throughout California’s Central Valley and in other countries such as Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and Mexico. I have also been a part of a group of students who started a club to discuss professionalism and ethics in dentistry – a club that eventually found its way into dental schools nationwide.

During my residency at Montefiore, I was able to further refine my knowledge of cosmetic dentistry, full mouth rehabilitation, and implant dentistry. I’ve also retained a desire for continuing education, always working to ensure that my practice is on the cutting edge of modern and cosmetic dentistry.

A New Kind of Dental Care

At Cedar Park Dental & Braces, we're not your typical dental office. Our philosophy centers on providing great dental care, a relaxing and comfortable environment, and affordable prices, ensuring every visit leaves you smiling brighter than when you arrived.

A Comforting Environment

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the difference. With amenities like a children's play area, refreshing beverages, and flat-screen TVs in each treatment room, your comfort is guaranteed.

Tailored Dental Care

We understand that every smile is unique. That's why our team of experts will sit down with you to craft a personalized dental care plan, ensuring your teeth stay healthy for years to come.

Affordable Excellence

We believe that everyone deserves access to top-notch dental care without breaking the bank. Our commitment to affordable pricing means you don't have to compromise on quality.

A Team That Cares

Our team members adhere to high standards and ideals, focusing on how we can make your visit better every day. We're passionate about helping you understand your dental health and how to maintain it.

Our Commitment to Your Smile

Your smile is a reflection of your happiness and health, and at Cedar Park Dental & Braces, we're dedicated to ensuring it shines brightly. Our comprehensive range of services means whether you need a routine check-up, braces, or more complex dental work, you're in expert hands. Our approachable team loves to educate our patients on the importance of dental procedures and how they contribute to overall wellness.

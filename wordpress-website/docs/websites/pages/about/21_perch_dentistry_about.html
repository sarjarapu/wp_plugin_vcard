Cosmetic & Implant Treatments

We love to craft unforgettable smiles. Whether you’ve dreamed of a stunning smile makeover or a fully restored smile, we offer tailored treatment options to show off your best to the world.

Cosmetic & Implant Treatments Include:

Lifetime Tooth Replacement With Dental Implants

Whether you are missing one tooth or multiple teeth, dental implants with our team offers a permanent and natural looking tooth replacement solution.

Composite Bonding

Designed to match your natural tooth color, composite bonding beautifully repairs chipped or cracked teeth, reduces gaps or spaces between teeth, and hides discoloration or faded areas on the tooth’s surface.

Invisalign® Orthodontic Solutions

Virtually invisible and removable, Invisalign’s clear aligners help patients achieve a beautifully straight smile without the challenge of traditional braces.

Dentures

Whether you need a partial set of dentures or a complete set, our team will assess your situation, make recommendations, answer all your questions, and deliver the option you choose

Professional Teeth Whitening

Faster. Fresher. Brighter. Our professional whitening treatment is more effective than over-the-counter options. Your refreshingly whiter smile awaits.

KOR® Teeth Whitening

Experience amazing teeth whitening results from the comfort of your own home with KOR® – widely recognized as the world’s most effective at-home teeth whitening system.

Porcelain Veneers

The quickest way to a Hollywood smile, porcelain veneers are perfectly crafted to fit your smile. In as little as two visits you could be showing off your stunning new smile.

Dental Bridge

If you are missing teeth, we can custom fit a dental bridge to fill a space that your tooth previously occupied.

Modern Technology

Welcome to the future of dentistry. From hassle-free digital impressions to high-definition 3D images and our own 3D printing lab, we invest in leading-edge solutions to meet your preventive and proactive dental needs.

Intraoral Camera

Enjoy gunk-free, full mouth digital impressions without all the hassle.
Intraoral digital images also provide our team with early detection and precision diagnosis for issues like decay, tartar, cavities, etc…

Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT)

Precision imaging without painful bitewings. Advanced CBCT technology provides a high-definition digital scan with 3-D images of your bones, teeth, nerves, sinuses; and structure of your head, face, neck and jaw.  

3D Dental Printer

No need to wait for the dental lab.
Our in-office, state-of-the-art 3D dental printer seamlessly turns digital images and impressions into precision-crafted oral appliances without a trip to the lab.

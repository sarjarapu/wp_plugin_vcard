Dental Hygiene and Teeth Cleaning

Cedar Park general dentist Dr. Jenna Jesko DDS recommends most people visit our office regularly for Austin dental hygiene appointments. Dental insurance typically covers a visit every six months, although we may suggest an appointment frequency based on your specific oral health needs. If you are overdue for a visit to the dentist, please call us today to schedule a cleaning and examination at our comfortable, luxurious dental office.

Visiting the Dentist for Dental Hygiene

During these visits our highly trained dental hygienist will thoroughly clean your teeth and gums, taking x-rays as needed and using an intraoral camera to allow you to see what’s happening inside of your mouth. Cleanings may be done with our Cavitron® microsonic instrument that comfortably cleans below the gum line to destroy the cell membranes of bacteria and prevent cavities.

Your regularly scheduled visits will also include a thorough examination from Cedar Park general dentist Dr. Jenna Jesko DDS will inspect every area of your mouth including the teeth, gums, tongue, lips, and jaw joint to detect early signs of common oral health issues such as gum disease, tooth decay, oral cancer, and TMJ. When detected early on, these things can often be effectively treated through minimally invasive techniques. Alternately, when not detected in time, some oral health issues can lead to chronic pain, the spread of infection and possibly the need for invasive treatments.

At-Home Care

Biannual visits are essential for optimal dental health but alone cannot prevent the causes of cavities and gum disease. To prevent oral health issues, you must take proper care of your teeth at home as well.

Your at-home routine should consist of:

  • Brushing with a soft bristled toothbrush and fluoride toothpaste after every meal and snack
  • Flossing once a day
  • Avoiding harmful substances such as sugary foods and beverages, and tobacco
Discussing proper at-home care will be part of your general dentistry visits at our office.

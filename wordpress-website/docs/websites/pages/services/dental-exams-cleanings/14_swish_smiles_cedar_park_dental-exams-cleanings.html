Treatment Options

Preventative Dental Treatments

Preventative dental care is the cornerstone of our practice, ensuring you maintain a healthy smile. Whether you're young or young at heart, our comprehensive dental care is tailored to meet every family member's needs. 

Comprehensive Dental Exams

Our comprehensive check ups go beyond the surface, utilizing digital X-rays, intraoral photos, and a 3D oral health scan to thoroughly assess your dental health. Our approach allows us to detect issues early on and create personalized treatment plans tailored to your needs.

Preventative Teeth Cleanings

Regular dental cleanings are essential for keeping your teeth and gums in optimal condition. Our gentle and effective preventative cleanings remove plaque and tartar buildup, while our fluoride treatments provide an added layer of protection against cavities.

Deep Cleanings

For patients with signs of gum disease or more advanced plaque buildup, our deep cleaning and gum therapy services can help restore your oral health. Our team offers scaling and root planing as well as laser gum therapy to effectively treat periodontal issues.

Sealants

Looking for a pain-free way to prevent cavities? Our sealant treatments provide a protective barrier over the biting surfaces of your teeth, reducing the risk of decay without any drilling involved.

Oral Cancer Screenings

Our comprehensive oral cancer screenings utilize advanced technology, including UV light detection with Oral ID, to identify any suspicious spots in your mouth, tongue, and throat.

Removable Appliances

Whether it’s to keep your teeth from grinding while you sleep or to maintain your newly straightened smile, we offer a range of removable appliances, such as mouthguards and retainers, to protect your smile and keep it looking its best.

Do you treat children?

Yes, at Swish Dental, we welcome patients of all ages, including children. Our skilled team specializes in offering gentle and considerate dental services tailored to children's needs. For more complex cases, we may make referrals to specialists who can address specific pediatric needs effectively.

Why do you recommend deep cleanings?

Deep cleanings help prevent heavy calculus buildup, bone loss, and inflamed gums. Unlike basic cleanings that focus on the surface level, deep cleanings address the areas underneath the gums, removing calculus and allowing your gums to breathe again. Deep cleanings help prevent tooth loss, heart disease, receding gums, and other serious dental issues.

Do I have to get X-Rays?

Yes, getting an X-ray is essential for a comprehensive understanding of your dental health. Our X-ray machines use minimal radiation for your safety. Not only is it a state requirement, but X-rays enable our providers to detect issues between teeth and ensure no hidden problems exist. The good news is that Swish Dental doesn't bill out-of-pocket costs for X-rays.

I’m pregnant -- Do I still need an X-Ray?

Yes, it's essential to have dental X-rays even during pregnancy. However, we will need clearance from your OBGYN before proceeding. Safety is our priority, and with proper clearance, we can ensure that your dental health is well taken care of during this special time.

Do you accept my insurance?

Swish Dental accepts a wide range of insurance plans to make your dental care affordable and accessible. For quick confirmation, use our insurance checker tool on our website to see if your insurance is among our accepted providers.

I don’t have insurance, can I still get care at Swish Dental?

Explore our budget-friendly Swish Smile Membership, which includes free exams, X-rays, cleanings, and a whitening session. You can also take advantage of our flexible payment plans through trusted partners or pay directly for specific services! Get in touch with us now for further details on the wide array of payment choices we offer.

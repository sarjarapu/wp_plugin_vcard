Family dentistry services are the cornerstone of a healthy smile. For the patients, it’s essential to practice good oral hygiene habits, including brushing and flossing, on a daily basis. Professional care is needed to keep your smile in good shape or to repair the damage that has occurred. Both home care and professional care are crucial to preserving your oral health, and neither should be neglected. Be sure to practice good oral hygiene habits every day, and see your dentist twice a year for checkups and professional cleanings.

Learn more

A straight smile is an asset in many ways. It boasts a person’s self-esteem, and also reduces the risk of injury to soft oral tissues and facilitates proper chewing without straining the jaw muscles. Unfortunately, though, many people develop misaligned teeth throughout the course of their development.

These problems can be corrected, and sometimes they can be addressed with a minimalistic approach early in the patient’s development.

Learn more

You may not be satisfied with the smile that mother nature gave you, but you’re not stuck with it. Thanks to the wonders of cosmetic dentistry, patients can enhance their natural smiles and eliminate any problematic aesthetic flaws. If there’s an aspect of your smile that bothers you, we encourage you to schedule a consultation with our team at Advanced Family Dentistry to learn about the treatment options that can give you the smile of your dreams.

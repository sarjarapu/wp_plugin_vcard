Dental Implants

Dental Implants

Transform your smile with our state-of-the-art dental implants. Designed for durability and a natural appearance, our implants are the perfect solution for missing teeth. Say goodbye to hiding your smile and hello to a new, confident you!

Book an Implant Consultation

What are dental implants?

Dental implants are a long-lasting solution for replacing missing teeth, providing a strong foundation for permanent or removable teeth. They offer the look and feel of natural teeth, restoring oral function and aesthetics.

Dental implants consist of:

  • A titanium post that integrates with your jawbone

  • A custom-made crown that matches your natural teeth

  • An abutment that connects the post to the crown

Treatment Options

Types of Dental Implants

Options for dental implants include single teeth, implant bridges, and dentures, depending on individual needs. They offer a long-term solution, preserving the jawbone and surrounding teeth. Whether you need a single tooth replacement or multiple tooth restoration, dental implants can be an effective option. The implant procedure is performed in stages, allowing for healing and proper integration with the jawbone. 

Single-Tooth Dental Implants

Single-tooth implants are an ideal choice for replacing one missing tooth. These implants blend seamlessly with your natural teeth, offering a strong and aesthetic replacement. The process involves the placement of a small post in the jaw, after a period of healing an implant extension is placed and final restoration is completed with a crown. 

Implant Bridges

For patients missing several teeth, implant bridges are a great solution. The procedure is similar to a single-tooth implant but allows for multiple teeth to be replaced at once. Typically two abutments (posts) are placed in the jaw and the new artificial teeth fill the gap between them. This allows for seamless and natural-looking restoration of your smile and bite.

Implant-Supported Dentures

Implant-supported dentures offer a solution for overcoming the limitations of traditional dentures. They securely latch onto titanium implants embedded in the jawbone, ensuring they stay in place during eating and talking. Unlike traditional dentures, this method promotes jawbone stimulation, preventing atrophy.

The Process

The Implant Procedure

The implant procedure is a comprehensive process that ensures the successful integration of dental implants for optimal oral health. The complete process occurs over multiple appointments and can take up to five months in total to allow for necessary healing. 

Book an Implant Consultation

Consultation and Treatment Planning

Your journey towards a new smile begins with a detailed consultation. Our experts assess your oral health, discuss your needs, and tailor a treatment plan specifically for you. We consider every aspect of your dental health and perform x-rays to ensure the best outcomes with our dental implants. 

Dental Implant Placement

Your implant placement appointment will occur two weeks after your consultation. During the procedure we surgically embed the titanium implant into your jawbone, setting the foundation for your new tooth. After this we ensure plenty of time for healing which allows the implants to fuse with the jawbone, creating a strong foundation for replacement teeth. 

The Abutment and Crown Placement Appointment

Following the healing period, you return for the placement of the abutment and crown. This is where your smile transformation is completed. Our dentists carefully fit the custom-designed crown onto the implant, ensuring a natural look and feel, perfectly blending with your smile. Once the custom crown is placed, maintaining oral health through proper hygiene and regular dental visits is essential.

How Much Do Dental Implants Cost?

The cost of dental implants can vary depending based on the number of implants required, and any additional procedures needed. The first consultation is free–our team will assess your case and determine the best next steps for you. 

Are Dental Implants Covered by Insurance?

Insurance coverage for dental implants varies depending on your insurance plan. Some plans may cover a portion of the cost, while others may not cover it at all. To determine your coverage, it's best to check with your insurance provider directly. You can also contact our office directly and our team can help you determine what coverage is available to you through your plan. 

How Long Does the Implant Process Take?

The dental implant process can up to five months to complete. It begins with a consultation and evaluation, followed by the placement of the implant. After this, there is a healing period of about 3 months before the final crown is placed.

Can I Get Financing for Dental Implants?

Yes, we offer various financing options for dental implants. Our patients can take advantage of in-house payment plans as well as financing through Affirm and CareCredit. These options make dental implants more affordable and accessible to those who need them. Call our office today and our team will be happy to answer any questions you may have about financing options. 

Does Swish do Full-Mouth Dental Implants?

Our personalized dental implant treatment plans include the option of full-mouth dental implants for patients with missing teeth or extensive dental damage. Our specialists will assess factors like bone density and overall health to determine if full-mouth implants are suitable for you. Schedule a consultation today to explore your options.

How Long Do Dental Implants Last?

Dental implants can have an impressive lifespan of several decades when properly cared for. Regular dental check-ups and cleanings are crucial to extending their longevity. Factors like oral hygiene habits, smoking, and medical conditions can impact how long dental implants last.

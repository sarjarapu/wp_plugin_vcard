## Content Analysis
### Writing Style & Tone
- Conversational-professional voice mixing "we" + "you" (Mosaic, My Cedar Park Dentist) proved most engaging.
- Paragraphs average 2–4 sentences; long-form pages (Prime Dental, Swish) use subheadings every 120–150 words for scanability.
- Power phrases recur: "permanent tooth replacement," "restore confidence," "custom treatment plan," "precision-guided." Competitors frequently repeat "Cedar Park, TX" in hero, H2s, and schema.

### Section Patterns
1. Hero: Service promise + CTA(s) (all pages).
2. "What are dental implants?" definitions (11/13 pages).
3. Benefits list tied to function, bone preservation, confidence (10 pages).
4. Process timeline + healing expectations (Mosaic, Prime Dental, Swish, Cedar Park Modern).
5. Candidate checklist + consultation invite.
6. Technology/technique (CBCT, guided surgery, sedation) callouts (7 pages).
7. Cost/insurance FAQs redirecting to consult (Swish, Cedar Park Dental & Braces, Prime Dental).
8. Testimonials/social proof (Buttercup, 512 Dentist, Cedar Park Premier) or "Why choose us" differentiators.
9. FAQ blocks covering pain, timeline, financing (8 pages).

### CTA Analysis
- Every competitor placed a phone + form CTA within hero.
- Strong mid-page CTAs after benefits/process with text such as "Schedule Implant Consultation," "Call [number]," "Book Online."
- Many (Bowcutt, 512 Dentist, Cedar Park Dental & Braces) embed CTA banners after objection-handling sections.
- Footer CTAs restate phone, address, and online booking link.

### SEO Analysis
- Titles follow `{Service} in Cedar Park, TX | {Practice}`.
- Meta descriptions (150–160 chars) highlight permanent solution + CTA.
- H1 always includes "Dental Implants" + "Cedar Park, TX" or "Cedar Park."
- Schema: Bowcutt + Mosaic include `MedicalProcedure`, `Article`, or `Dentist` JSON-LD; replicating Aviva's home/about schema footprint with a `MedicalProcedure` block is expected.
- Internal linking: Several pages link to other services (Swish cross-sells Invisalign; Cedar Park Premier cross-links sedation). We should link to Aviva's restorative or contact pages.

### Technology & Features
- CBCT/digital planning (New Hope, Cedar Park Premier, Bowcutt) builds trust.
- Sedation/comfort and bone graft/sinus lift readiness (Buttercup, Bowcutt, My Cedar Park Dentist, Mosaic) reassure anxious patients.
- Same-day/emergency placement (My Cedar Park Dentist, Cedar Park Dental & Braces) adds urgency.
- Financing + insurance acceptance appear on 10/13 pages; highlight Aviva's PPO acceptance + membership options from existing copy.

## Implementation Guidelines
- **Tone:** Confident, empathetic, jargon-light; alternate "we" + "you" to keep it personal.
- **Length:** 1,200–1,400 words per workflow guardrail; keep sections 2–4 sentences each, with strategic bullet lists.
- **Structure:** Reuse `.hero`, `.section-intro`, `.section-text`, `.features-grid`, `.contact-info`, `.cta-button` classes from home/about to maintain layout parity.
- **SEO:** Title `<title>Dental Implants in Cedar Park, TX | Aviva Dental Care</title>`, meta description 155 chars, H1 matches title, include Cedar Park in at least three H2s, add internal links to `/services/` and `/contact/`.
- **Schema:** Include `MedicalProcedure` JSON-LD referencing Aviva Dental Care as provider and summarizing benefits; reuse formatting from home/about schema blocks.
- **Imagery/alt text:** Reference smile outcomes or technology with descriptive alt tags (no stock references needed, just textual descriptions for now).
- **Internal Links:** At minimum, link to `services`, `contact`, and optionally `about` or `virtual-tour` sections for technology proof.
- **Pricing language:** Emphasize consultation-only estimates, PPO acceptance, membership plans, and financing partners—never list dollar amounts.

## Pricing Information Handling
- **Rule:** Never quote specific fees or discounts.
- **Messaging template:** "Investment varies based on bone density, number of implants, and restoration design. We'll review your options, PPO benefits, and financing during a consultation." Redirect readers to contact Aviva for personalized estimates.
- **CTA pairing:** Place cost language within FAQ alongside "Schedule a consultation" button to convert interest into appointments.

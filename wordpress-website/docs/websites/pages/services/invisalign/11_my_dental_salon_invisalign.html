What are the benefits of Invisalign?

Invisalign has been around for decades and has remained one of the most popular clear aligner brands on the market. Patients tend to love Invisalign for the following reasons:

  • Clear and Virtually Unnoticeable
  • Straighten Teeth Quickly and Effectively
  • Comfortable to Wear for Hours at a Time
  • Easy to Remove and Clean
  • No Food or Activity Restrictions

¿Cuáles son los beneficios de Invisalign?

Invisalign ha estado presente por décadas y sigue siendo una de las marcas más populares de alineadores transparentes. Los principales beneficios de un tratamiento invisalign son los siguientes:

  • Son transparentes y fáciles de quitar
  • Los resultados empiezan a aparecer en semanas.
  • Son cómodos de usar por tiempo prolongado.

How do you take care of Invisalign aligners?

Keeping your aligners clean is essential if you want them to remain clear and discreet. You should clean them with liquid soap or Invisalign’s cleaning system and a soft-bristle toothbrush at least once per day. You can also soak your aligners in Invisalign cleaning crystals to ensure that any harmful bacteria is removed. Always rinse them off after taking them out and after cleaning them thoroughly. We also recommend storing your aligners in their case whenever they are not being worn so that they do not get accidentally lost or thrown away.

¿Cómo cuidar de tus alineadores Invisalign?

Es muy importante mantener tus alineadores limpios. Se deben limpiar con jabón líquido o pasta de dientes y un cepillo de dientes de cerdas suaves, por lo menos una vez al día.

Siempre se deben enjuagar después de limpiarlos profundamente. También recomendamos almacenar tus alineadores en su estuche cuando no los estés usando, para prevenir que puedas perderlos o dañarlos accidentalmente.

Is Invisalign painful?

Your teeth are not used to being shifted, so once you begin wearing your aligners between 20 and 22 hours per day, you may experience some pain or discomfort. However, it shouldn’t be anything that over-the-counter pain medication can’t relieve. You can also apply dental wax to areas of the aligner that are causing soreness. When you need to switch to the next aligner in the series, doing so before going to sleep can also allow the aligner to make its primary adjustments while you are asleep, which can help minimize discomfort.

What issues can Invisalign treat?

Invisalign can treat a variety of dental issues. Though the clear aligners are discreet and comfortable, they are just as effective as traditional orthodontic appliances. Whether you have a minor or tough case of correction, Invisalign aligners can treat conditions including gaps between teeth, crowding, and bite issues like open, cross, or overbites. However, only an Invisalign consultation with our team can determine if Invisalign is the right treatment option to correct your dental issues. Even if Invisalign is not best for you, a properly aligned smile is a beautiful one and can result in healthier teeth and gums!

Are there any food restrictions with Invisalign?

Many patients are happy to hear that Invisalign does not pose any food or beverage restrictions. While this is the case, patients should always remove aligners when eating to prevent damage to aligner material. After taking off and putting the aligners back on, we highly recommend brushing and flossing your teeth or at least rinsing off the aligners thoroughly to keep saliva and plaque build-up to a minimum. Remember to put your aligners back on after eating so treatment can stay on track!

What can I expect after Invisalign treatment?

The last appointment of your Invisalign treatment is no doubt the most exciting. However, after your treatment, we recommend you use a retainer to maintain your results and prevent your teeth from shifting. Caring for a retainer isn’t that much different than wearing aligners, and over time, we may suggest you only wear the retainer overnight. Ultimately, the amount of time and what you can expect after Invisalign will vary depending on your treatment. We will discuss your situation specifically when you’re nearing the end of treatment and create your custom retainer to fit comfortably!

Preguntas Frequentes

¿Qué problemas puede eliminar Invisalign?

Los alineadores de Invisalign pueden tratar condiciones que incluyen espacios entre los dientes, apiñamiento, y problemas de mordida. Es importante una consulta para determinar si Invisalign es la opción de tratamiento adecuada para resolver sus problemas dentales.

¿Hay limitaciones de comida con Invisalign?

Invisalign no requiere ninguna restricción de alimentos o bebidas, sin embargo, el paciente siempre debe quitarse los alineadores al comer para evitar que se dañe el material de los alineadores.

¿Qué esperar después del tratamiento Invisalign?

La última cita de su tratamiento Invisalign es la más emocionante. Después de su tratamiento, es necesario utilizar un retenedor para mantener sus resultados y prevenir que sus dientes se se muevan.

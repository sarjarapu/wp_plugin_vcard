## Content Analysis
### Writing Style & Tone
- Conversational-expert: "we" and "you" voice, confident but warm, 2-3 sentence paragraphs.
- Pain-point led intros (“severe tooth pain”, “confidence when you smile”).
- Technical vocabulary sprinkled with plain-language translations (porcelain crown = “tooth-colored cap”).
- Bilingual reassurance (My Dental Salon) and inclusive phrasing (“families around Cedar Park and Leander”).

### Section Patterns
1. **Hero:** Title/H1 + 2-sentence hook + dual CTA (call + schedule).
2. **What is a dental crown / bridge?** definitions + visuals.
3. **When you need crowns:** chips, decay, root canals, implants, cosmetic upgrades.
4. **Materials & technology:** porcelain/ziconia/ceramic, CAD/CAM, digital scans, partner labs.
5. **Process overview:** consultation → prep → temp → final placement; highlight same-day vs lab-made timeline.
6. **Comfort & safety:** anesthesia, sedation options, rubber dam/isolation, infection control.
7. **Results & longevity:** lifespan, maintenance tips, avoid hard foods, regular hygiene.
8. **Combined solutions:** bridges, implants, full-mouth rehab.
9. **Cost & insurance guidance:** emphasize individualized pricing; mention financing, membership plan, insurance assistance.
10. **Testimonials / social proof** (quotes from Cedar Park patients, star ratings) + CTA.
11. **FAQ block** answering pain, duration, materials, aftercare, coverage.

### CTA Analysis
- Primary verbs: “Schedule Online”, “Book Appointment”, “Call (512) XXX-XXXX”, “Request Consultation”.
- Placement: hero, after benefits, before/after process, final CTA block; best pages show both phone + form CTA every ~400 words.
- Language synergy: CTAs often include value (“Reserve your custom crown visit”, “Use your benefits before they expire”).

### SEO Analysis
- Titles stay under 60 chars and include location (e.g., “Dental Crowns in Cedar Park, TX | Practice”).
- Meta descriptions emphasize benefit + CTA (“Rebuild cracked teeth with porcelain crowns in Cedar Park. Call today”).
- H1 mirrors title; H2s include long-tails ("What to expect during a dental crown in Cedar Park").
- Schema usage: Bowcutt + Prime Dental embed WebPage/Article + LocalBusiness; Aviva already uses rich metadata but lacks MedicalProcedure + FAQ.
- Internal links: competitors link to implants, fillings, emergency care, financing.

### Technology & Features Emphasized
- Porcelain, zirconia, e.max, and metal-free options.
- CEREC/same-day crowns, in-office milling, temporary crowns vs permanent timeline.
- Digital impressions (iTero, 3D scanners) to avoid trays.
- Sedation dentistry, gentle anesthetic delivery, rubber dam isolation.
- Financing, insurance maximization, membership plans, bilingual team support.

## Implementation Guidelines
- **Content type:** Educational + persuasive, ~1,250 words, 2-4 sentences per paragraph, frequent subheadings.
- **Layout:** Mirror `home.html`/`about.html` containers (`.container`, `.hero`, `.section-intro`, `.section-text`, `.contact-info`, `.cta-row`). Use alternating light/dark sections for rhythm.
- **Voice:** First-person plural (“we”) with direct address (“you”), confident and neighborly.
- **CTA placement:** hero, after benefits, after process, after financing, final footer-style block. Provide phone `(512) 852-8528` + `/contact` + `/book-appointment/` links. Include Spanish prompt CTA.
- **SEO:**
  - Title: `Dental Crowns in Cedar Park, TX | Aviva Dental Care`
  - Meta description: 155-char summary referencing smile restoration + consultation.
  - H1/H2 include "Dental Crowns" + "Cedar Park" variations.
  - Include internal links to `services/dental-implants`, `services/root-canal`, `services/dental-exams-cleanings`, `/contact`.
  - Add MedicalProcedure + FAQ schema.
- **Pricing Language:** avoid dollar figures; use “Exact investment determined after a personalized consultation. We review insurance benefits and flexible payments before treatment begins.”
- **Compliance:** emphasize consultation, no guarantees, mention that outcomes vary.
- **Bilingual:** dedicate short Spanish paragraph + CTA (“¿Prefiere atención en español? Nuestro equipo bilingüe está listo…”).
- **Accessibility:** descriptive alt text for imagery, ordered lists for process, FAQs with semantic headings.

## Pricing Information Handling
- Never list fees or discounts. Use language such as: “Because every tooth and material selection is unique, we finalize your investment after an in-person exam. Our team reviews insurance coverage, membership savings, and financing so you can choose the best plan before we begin.” Always steer readers to call or schedule for personalized pricing.

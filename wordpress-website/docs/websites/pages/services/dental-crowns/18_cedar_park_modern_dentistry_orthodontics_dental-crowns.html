What is a Dental Crown?

Dental crowns, crafted from a variety of materials, are custom-fitted caps designed to restore and strengthen damaged teeth. Whether you have a cracked tooth, a severe decay, or a large filling that needs replacement, crowns can provide durable protection. With CEREC® same-day crowns, a procedure that otherwise requires multiple visits, can be accomplished in just one. 

 

4 Benefits of Dental Crowns

 

Strengthen Teeth

Dental crowns provide added support to weakened or damaged teeth, preventing further deterioration. 

Natural Look

Achieve a more natural-looking smile by covering discolored, misshapen, or damaged teeth. 

Long-Term Solution

Durable and long-lasting, dental crowns offer protection and stability to your teeth for years to come.

 

Flexible Options

Dental crowns offer tailored treatments for both cosmetic and functional issues to meet your dental needs effectively.

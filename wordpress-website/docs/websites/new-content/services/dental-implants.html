During your consultation we review medical history, medications, and home-care habits, then capture a CBCT scan to evaluate bone density, sinuses, and nerves. Dental Implant FAQs

Still researching implants in Cedar Park? Start here, then reach out‚Äîwe love answering questions.

Do dental implants hurt?

Placement is performed with local anesthetic plus nitrous oxide or oral sedation if requested. You‚Äôll feel pressure, not pain, and most guests return to normal routines within a day or two. We text check-ins, recommend gentle meals, and provide medication guidance.

How long does the process take?

Simple cases can be restored in three to four months; full-arch and grafted cases may take six months or more. We always provide provisional teeth so you can smile and eat while the implant integrates.

What if I have bone loss or wear dentures?

CBCT scans show whether you need bone grafting, sinus lifts, or mini-implants to stabilize dentures. We routinely help long-time denture wearers upgrade to snap-in or fixed bridges.

How much do dental implants cost?

Pricing depends on the number of implants, biomaterials, and whether grafting or sedation is required. Instead of stock quotes, we prepare a customized estimate after your exam and review insurance, the Aviva Wellness Plan, and financing options with you.

How do I care for implants?

Brush twice daily, clean around implants with floss or interdental brushes, and visit Aviva twice a year for professional maintenance. We use digital photos to monitor tissue health and share any home-care tweaks you may need.

What Happens During Your Dental Filling Appointment

Composite fillings at Aviva Dental Care are efficient yet unrushed. We borrow the best ideas we saw across 15 competitor pages—clear timelines, comfort checkpoints, and post-op coaching—and layer them with Aviva's personal touch.

  1. Digital diagnostics & smile goals. We review symptoms, inspect existing fillings, and capture digital X-rays or Diagnodent readings so you can see the decay right on the screen.
  2. Comfort customization. Topical anesthetic, buffered local anesthesia, and optional nitrous oxide or cozy amenities keep the tooth numb and your mind relaxed. We isolate the tooth with Isolite/rubber dam barriers to protect your airway.
  3. Conservative prep. Dr. Arjarapu removes only the damaged enamel, disinfects the area with antimicrobial rinse, and lines deeper fillings with bio-compatible materials for added insulation.
  4. Layered bonding & sculpting. We place the composite in layers, cure each one with high-output LED lights, then sculpt the grooves so your bite feels natural immediately.
  5. Bite check & aftercare coaching. We polish the restoration, verify your bite in motion, and review sensitivity timelines plus tips for protecting the new filling at home.

Know When to Schedule a Filling in Cedar Park

Warning Signs

Sensitivity to sweets, sudden twinges with cold drinks, food catching between teeth, or floss shredding are all indicators that decay or a small fracture is forming. Call as soon as you feel a change—we can often plan same-week visits so damage stays minimal.

Time to Replace an Old Filling

Dark lines along the edge of a silver filling, a chipped composite, or a filling that's more than 10 years old can leak bacteria. We evaluate margins with digital imaging, discuss SMART-inspired replacement when needed, and show you options ranging from direct composite to porcelain inlays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Patients across Cedar Park asked similar questions while researching fillings. Here are the answers—then call us for guidance tailored to your tooth.

Do dental fillings hurt?

We start with topical anesthetic, use buffered local anesthesia, and offer nitrous oxide or comfort amenities on request. Most guests feel only light vibration, and we check in before every step.

How long do composite fillings last?

High-quality composites typically perform 7–12 years, and even longer when paired with great home care, nightguards for clenching, and regular exams. We inspect every filling at your preventive visits.

Can I eat right after my appointment?

Wait until the numbness disappears so you do not bite your cheek or tongue. Once sensation returns, you can chew as usual—composites are fully cured before you leave.

When should I replace an old filling?

Schedule a visit if you notice cracks, dark lines along the edge, sensitivity when biting, or if the filling is older than a decade. We will evaluate the seal and recommend repair or replacement.

How much do fillings cost?

Fees depend on the tooth, the surfaces involved, and your insurance benefits. We review personalized estimates, offer membership savings, and invite you to schedule a consultation so we can plan exactly what you need.

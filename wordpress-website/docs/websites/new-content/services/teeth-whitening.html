Your Whitening Visit, Step by Step

  1. Consultation & shade mapping. We review goals, inspect restorations, take photos, and select a target shade that complements your smile and skin tone.
  2. Comfort customization. Desensitizing polish, Vitamin E, and optional nitrous or warm blankets are offered before we begin. We also provide bilingual explanations so every guest feels informed.
  3. Precision isolation. Soft-tissue barriers and retractors protect lips and gums so only enamel contacts the whitening gel.
  4. LED activation cycles. We apply fresh gel, activate it with Zoom WhiteSpeed light in 15-minute intervals, and monitor sensitivity after each round.
  5. Hydration & take-home kit. A fluoride or nano-hydroxyapatite treatment calms the enamel, then we deliver custom trays plus booster gel with printed care instructions (English & Spanish).
  6. Maintenance plan. We book follow-up touch-ups, share our 48-hour "color-safe" menu, and text reminders before big events.

Who's a Candidate for Teeth Whitening?

Ideal Candidates

Adults and teens with stain build-up from coffee, tea, wine, or aging; anyone preparing for photos, interviews, or aligner treatment; patients with healthy enamel and gums.

When We Press Pause

Pregnancy/breastfeeding, untreated cavities, active gum disease, or teeth with deep cracks require care first. Existing crowns/veneers do not change color, so replacements may be needed afterward.

Time It Right

Whiten after cleanings, before finalizing veneers or Invisalign attachments, and at least two weeks before composite bonding so we can match your new shade precisely.

Teeth Whitening FAQs

Still researching whitening in Cedar Park? These answers borrow the best practices we observed across 12 competitor pages and tailor them to Aviva's comfort-first approach.

How fast will I see results?

Most guests see their final shade before leaving the operatory. Zoom WhiteSpeed delivers three 15-minute cycles for an instant lift, and custom trays keep the glow going at home.

Will my teeth feel sensitive?

Some people notice temporary "zingers," but we minimize them with desensitizing polish, fluoride, Relief ACP gel, and by tailoring light exposure. Let us know your history and we'll prep in advance.

Can whitening change crowns or fillings?

Porcelain crowns, veneers, and composite fillings do not bleach. We'll review which teeth might need replacement or cosmetic polish after whitening so everything matches.

How much does professional whitening cost?

Fees depend on the protocol (in-office, combo, or take-home only) and your existing restorations. We review personalized estimates, membership savings, and financing options during your consultation-never online.

Is whitening safe during pregnancy or breastfeeding?

We recommend waiting until after pregnancy and while not nursing. Book a consultation anyway-we'll map out timing, provide hygiene tips, and reserve a spot for when you get the green light.

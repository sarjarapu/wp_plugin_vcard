What Are Dental Crowns & Bridges?

A dental crown is a custom, tooth-shaped cover that protects a weakened tooth from further cracking, restores the exact height you need to chew comfortably, and seals out bacteria after large fillings or root canal therapy. The American Dental Association notes crowns are the most predictable way to reinforce teeth that have lost 50% or more of their structure.

A dental bridge uses crowns on the teeth (or implants) around a gap to support a lifelike replacement tooth. Bridges are ideal when you want to avoid a removable partial denture or when bone isn‚Äôt ready for implants yet. Our team photographs your smile, evaluates your bite, and helps you decide when a single crown, bridge, or implant crown is the best path.

Porcelain Crowns

Beautiful translucency for front teeth. Layered ceramics mimic enamel and resist stains from coffee or wine.

Zirconia & BruxZir

Ultra-strong, metal-free options built for molars and grinders who need maximum durability.

Same-Day & Lab-Crafted

We offer expedited chairside milling for urgent cases and partner with elite CAD/CAM labs for complex esthetic work.

Most of our Cedar Park, Leander, Round Rock, and Austin patients choose porcelain or zirconia crowns for their front teeth because they reflect light like natural enamel. When a back molar needs maximum durability, we can still discuss e.max hybrid ceramics or high-noble metal designs‚ÄîDr. Arjarapu walks you through the pros and cons so you always know why a specific material is recommended. Regardless of the choice, every crown is nickel-free and bonded with high-strength resin cements to eliminate gray margins at the gumline.

What Happens During Your Dental Crown Experience

Crowns typically take two visits (or one if we expedite) and every step is designed to keep you informed, comfortable, and confident about the result.

  1. Digital diagnostics & smile planning. We capture photos, X-rays, and intraoral scans, review existing restorations, and map out goals for color, translucency, and bite support.
  2. Comfort customization. Topical + buffered anesthetic, nitrous oxide, and comfort amenities keep the tooth numb while rubber dam or Isolite isolation protects your airway.
  3. Tooth preparation & build-up. Dr. Arjarapu removes decay or old material, rebuilds missing tooth structure, and shapes the tooth conservatively to maximize strength.
  4. Digital scan & temporary crown. We scan or impress the prep, place a contoured temporary crown, and transmit files to our CAD/CAM lab (or mill in-house when available).
  5. Delivery & bite refinement. About 1‚Äì2 weeks later (or same-day for select cases) we try in the final crown, check contacts and occlusion, and bond it permanently before polishing.

Frequently Asked Questions

These are the questions Cedar Park patients ask most before moving forward with crowns. Still wondering about something? Call us‚Äîwe love explaining the details.

Do dental crowns hurt?

No. We start with topical anesthetic, buffer local anesthesia so it warms to body temperature, and offer nitrous, blankets, and sedation partners if you need more support. You may feel vibration, but not pain.

How long will my crown last?

Porcelain and zirconia crowns routinely last 10‚Äì15 years (often longer) when paired with great home care, nightguards for grinders, and regular hygiene visits. We inspect margins at every exam and let you know if anything changes.

What materials do you use?

We select porcelain, zirconia, e.max, or porcelain-fused-to-zirconia depending on location, esthetics, and bite. Dr. A reviews shade guides and durability specs with you before we prep.

Can you do same-day crowns?

Most cases involve a temporary crown and a final visit 1‚Äì2 weeks later, but we can expedite deliveries or mill same-day options for select teeth and emergencies. Either way, you never leave without a comfortable temporary.

How much do dental crowns cost?

Fees depend on the tooth, material, whether a root canal or build-up is needed, and your insurance benefits. Instead of posting prices online, we review personalized estimates after your consultation and outline insurance, membership, and financing options.

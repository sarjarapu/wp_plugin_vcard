How Invisalign Works at Aviva Dental Care

Borrowing the best structure we saw across top-performing Invisalign providers, we make every phase transparent, tech-forward, and comfortable.

  1. Consultation & digital scan. We capture iTero®-style scans, photos, and X-rays, then discuss lifestyle goals, upcoming events, and any restorative work we should stage around treatment.
  2. 3D smile simulation. Dr. Arjarapu maps tooth movement with ClinCheck®-style software so you can preview each stage, understand attachment placement, and approve the plan.
  3. Attachment placement & first aligners. We place SmartForce® attachments or precision cuts as needed, deliver your starter aligners, and Coach you through wear, storage, and switching cadence.
  4. Progress check-ins. You will swap trays every 7–10 days, log photos through our secure portal (or text them if that's easier), and visit the office roughly every 6–8 weeks for refinements.
  5. Refinements & retainers. When aligners finish, we polish attachments, take new scans for Vivera® retainers, and outline how often to wear them so your results stay locked in.

Need travel accommodations, remote check-ins, or Spanish-language instructions? We have you covered. Nitrous oxide, topical anesthetic, and entertainment screens mirror the comfort perks highlighted on our about page, so even attachment appointments feel easy.

Benefits of Invisalign Compared to Braces

Invisalign isn’t just about straight teeth; it’s about fitting advanced orthodontics into your lifestyle. Here’s what patients enjoy most:

  • Discreet confidence. Virtually invisible trays keep you camera-ready for weddings, graduations, and daily selfies.
  • Removable freedom. Enjoy your favorite foods and brush or floss normally without threading around brackets.
  • Comfort and predictability. SmartTrack® material applies gentle, even pressure, while ClinCheck® 3D planning shows the projected outcome before you begin.
  • Fewer in-office visits. Remote photo check-ins and bundled aligner deliveries mean less time away from school or work.
  • Whole-health benefits. Straight teeth are easier to clean, lowering the risk of gum inflammation, TMJ discomfort, and enamel wear—all outcomes we monitor at every visit.

Life with Aligners: Wear, Care & Retention

Wear your aligners 20–22 hours per day, remove them only for meals, coffee, and brushing, and rinse them with cool water before popping them back in. We will show you how to brush attachments gently, provide travel cases and cleaning crystals, and coordinate professional cleanings so your gums stay healthy throughout treatment.

When treatment ends, we polish attachments, scan for Vivera or in-house retainers, and coach you through night-time wear to protect your investment. Traveling soon? Have a refinement question? Text or call us—our same-day emergency policy applies to aligner hiccups, too.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still curious about Invisalign in Cedar Park? Start here, then call or text us if you need more details.

How long does Invisalign treatment take?

Most Aviva patients finish in 6–18 months, matching the timelines you’ll see from other advanced Invisalign centers. The exact length depends on your goals, bite, and how consistently you wear aligners. We revisit your projected timeline at every visit.

How many hours per day should I wear aligners?

Plan on 20–22 hours daily. Remove trays only for meals, coffee, and brushing. We provide switching calendars, text reminders, and bilingual instructions so staying on schedule feels effortless.

Will Invisalign hurt?

You may feel light pressure for a day or two whenever you switch trays. OTC pain relievers, chilled aligner chewies, and our comfort menu (warm towels, Netflix, nitrous oxide) keep attachments and refinements relaxing.

Can I eat or drink with aligners in?

Stick to water while aligners are in place. Remove them for meals and hot drinks, brush or rinse before reinserting, and store them in a case so you never misplace a tray at school, work, or the gym.

Does insurance cover Invisalign?

Many PPO plans include lifetime orthodontic benefits. We verify coverage, file claims, and walk you through flexible payments, the Aviva Wellness Plan, CareCredit, or Sunbit options during your consultation.

What if I lose or crack an aligner?

Call us immediately. We can provide a replacement, advise you to wear the previous or next tray, and schedule a same-day visit if an attachment needs smoothing. Fast support keeps your timeline on track.

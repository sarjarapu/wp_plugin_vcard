Frequently Asked Questions

If you do not see your question answered here, please call or send us a message—we are happy to help.

How often should I schedule dental exams and cleanings?

Most people visit every six months, but we personalize recall intervals based on your cavity risk, gum health, orthodontics, pregnancy, or medical conditions. We will recommend the cadence that keeps you healthiest.

What happens during a preventive visit?

Each visit includes digital imaging, periodontal charting, an oral cancer screening, a comprehensive exam with Dr. Arjarapu, and a gentle cleaning plus home-care coaching. You will leave knowing exactly where your oral health stands.

Will the cleaning hurt?

Most guests feel only mild pressure, yet we have topical numbing gels, ultrasonic instruments, and relaxation amenities ready if you are sensitive. Tell us what you need and we will adjust.

Does insurance cover preventive visits?

Preventive care is covered by almost every PPO plan. Our concierge verifies benefits beforehand and explains any estimated copay. If you are not using insurance, ask about the Aviva Wellness Plan.

What if I need a deep cleaning?

If we spot signs of periodontal disease, we will review imaging and measurements with you, outline scaling and root planing recommendations, and discuss maintenance visits to protect your progress.

Results, Longevity & What to Expect After Bonding

After bonding we review home-care tips, provide a non-abrasive polishing kit, and text you within 48 hours to be sure everything feels perfect. Mild temperature sensitivity is normal for a few days; most guests return to coffee, social events, and workouts the same afternoon.

Porcelain veneers typically last 10‚Äì15 years, and composite veneers average 5‚Äì7 years depending on habits. Routine hygiene visits let us polish without dulling the glaze, document changes with photos, and reinforce nightguard wear if you clench or grind.

  • First 48 hours: Stick to soft foods, avoid biting directly into hard items, and brush gently with a soft bristle brush.
  • First 2 weeks: Return for a bite check and let us adjust any sounds, speech nuances, or edges that need refinement.
  • Ongoing: Wear your nightguard, use non-whitening toothpaste, and pair veneers with professional cleanings twice per year.

Porcelain veneers average 10‚Äì15 years and composite veneers 5‚Äì7 years depending on habits. Wearing a nightguard, avoiding nail biting, and keeping up with hygiene visits protects your investment.

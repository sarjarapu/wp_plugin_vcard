Advanced Technology, Comfortable Experience

Our Cedar Park dental office is fully equipped with advanced dental technologies to ensure procedures are pain-free, comfortable, and long-lasting. These technologies help Dr. Arjarapu view your complete dental profile in unprecedented detail, enabling early detection of oral complications with remarkable accuracy.

Low-Radiation Digital X-Rays

Our digital X-ray system decreases your radiation exposure by an astounding 90% compared to traditional X-rays. This advanced imaging technology provides clearer images for more accurate diagnosis while prioritizing your safety and long-term health.

Intraoral Camera

See exactly what we see. Our intraoral digital camera enables us to show you precisely what's happening in your mouth, helping you better understand your dental needs and treatment options. This technology enhances patient involvement in the diagnosis process.

Cavity Detection

Advanced cavity detection technology allows us to identify decay early, often before it becomes visible to the naked eye. Early detection means less invasive treatments and better outcomes for your long-term oral health.

3D Imaging

Our advanced 3D imaging technology provides comprehensive views of your oral structures, enabling precise treatment planning for dental implants, root canals, and other complex procedures. This technology allows for more accurate diagnoses and better treatment outcomes.

Digital Impressions

Say goodbye to messy, uncomfortable traditional impression materials. Our digital impression technology creates precise 3D models of your teeth quickly and comfortably, improving accuracy for crowns, bridges, and other restorations while enhancing your comfort during the process.

Eco-Friendly Sterilization

We autoclave equipment at very high temperatures to ensure no bacteria, spores, or harmful organisms survive. We don't use cold sterilization chemicals that are harmful to the environment. Anything that has even a remote possibility of contamination is sterilized or discarded after each visit. Your safety and environmental responsibility are both priorities.
